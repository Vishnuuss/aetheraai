Common Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Most "Build" projects ship in 4–8 weeks. Complex systems with multiple integrations may take longer. We always start with a prototype in the first 1–2 weeks so you can validate before committing full budget.

What if I'm not satisfied with the result?

We offer a 30-day warranty on all Build projects. If something isn't working as specified, we fix it at no additional cost. Our goal is a system you actually use — not one that collects dust.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. Build projects can be split into milestones: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery. Audit and Iterate plans are paid monthly.

What's included in the warranty?

Bugs, critical failures, and issues directly caused by our implementation. It doesn't cover scope changes, new integrations, or issues from upstream dependencies (like third-party API changes).

Can I switch from Iterate to Build for new features?

Absolutely. Many clients start with Iterate and promote features to a full Build engagement when they outgrow the scope. Just reach out — we'll scope it out and give you a fixed price.

Need something custom?

Large-scale deployments, multi-team rollouts, and custom SLA requirements — let's talk.
